About Us

Investors lack trust and understanding of traditional sustainability investments.

SeedShift empowers investors to explore, understand, and invest in projects that offer transparent data and attractive returns.

Features

What makes SeedShift better

Our projects are carefully selected and screened by our team. We provide detailed and transparent reports to ensure your investments yield both financial and social returns. We want to inspire you to be part of their journey towards sustainability.

Transparency

Building transparent data into SeedShift is one of our most important goals. We aim for our investors to have a comprehensive understanding of their investments and the ability to independently verify them. This is why we work directly with project owners so that we can obtain the highest fidelity data possible.

Security

We utilize cutting edge decentralized fund management systems to ensure that funds only go to the right people. All funds are held in custody until projects meet pre-established roadmap milestones. If these milestones are not met, all funds are returned to the investors.

Variety

Our global climate is a balance of countless factors, but we have disrupted this natural equilibrium. SeedShift provides investors access to many different areas of sustainability projects, so that they can find the one they are most passionate about.

AI Integration

We believe that leveraging AI for data analytics yields the most efficient results for our investors. Using this system gives us access to the global network of climate data and reporting, providing the highest quality data analysis.
